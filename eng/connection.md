## 📡 Phase II: Signal Established (Server IP)

Once your client is configured, use the coordinates below to locate our world.

::: tip SERVER COORDINATES:
### `dxb-1.zeyroncloud.com:19139`
:::

**Instructions:**
1. Launch Minecraft with the **Cobblemon** profile.
2. Navigate to **Multiplayer** > **Add Server**.
3. Input the address above and save.

## 🔑 Phase III: Security Clearance (The Whitelist)

Our world is a protected frontier. To bypass the security perimeter, you must link your identity through our Discord Command Center.

**The Process:**
1. Join our [**Official Discord**](https://discord.gg/TDBcpqBxvZ).
2. Navigate to the `#clearance` channel.
3. Type your IGN (In-Game Name) to request whitelist access.
