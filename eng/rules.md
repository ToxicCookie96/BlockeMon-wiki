# 📜 The Warden's Code

Welcome to **BlockéMon**. You are no longer just a trainer; you are a pioneer.  
To ensure the frontier remains a land of discovery for everyone, all adventurers must follow these protocols.

---

## 🛡️ I. The Code of Conduct
*Professionalism and respect are the foundations of our community.*

* **The Golden Rule:** Treat fellow pioneers with respect. Toxicity, harassment, or discriminatory behavior will result in an immediate exile.
* **Communication:** Keep the airwaves clear. No spamming, advertising, or NSFW content in global channels.
* **Conflict Resolution:** If a dispute arises, document evidence and submit a report via our **Discord Command Center**.

## ⚔️ II. Frontier Survival (In-Game)
*Protecting the integrity of the world is every player's responsibility.*

* **Standard of Fair Play:** The use of modified clients, x-rays, or exploitation of glitches is strictly prohibited. We play on a level playing field.
* **Property Rights:** Griefing or looting established bases is a high-level offense. If it isn't yours, don't touch it.
* **Combat Engagement:** Open-world PvP is **Consensual Only**. Do not engage other players unless a duel has been formally accepted.

## 🐾 III. The Natural Order
*Rules for a balanced ecosystem.*

* **Public Infrastructure:** Healing stations, Waystones, and PC terminals are protected assets. Do not break or hoard them.
* **First Discovery:** Rare and Legendary Pokémon belong to the explorer who initiated the encounter first. 
* **Fair Trade:** All commerce must be transparent. Scams will lead to permanent trade restrictions.

## 🏗️ IV. Settling the Land
*Building a sustainable world.*

* **Permanent Settlements:** You are encouraged to stake your own claim and build original structures rather than occupying generated villages indefinitely.
* **Technical Optimization:** Avoid "Infinite Loop" redstone machines or massive mob farms that impact server performance.
* **Environmental Impact:** Avoid leaving floating trees or deep, unmarked pits in the wilderness.

---

::: danger FINAL ACKNOWLEDGMENT
By proceeding to the deployment instructions, you officially agree to abide by **The Warden's Code**. Violations of these protocols will result in disciplinary action by the Dispatch Team.
:::
